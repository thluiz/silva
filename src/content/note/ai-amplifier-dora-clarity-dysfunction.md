**00:10:07** — AI as Amplifier: DORA Report on Clarity vs Dysfunction

Annie references the DORA report to argue that the junior hiring crisis didn't start with AI — AI is merely an amplifier. As the report states, AI magnifies your strengths and your clarity, or your dysfunction. In this case, it's magnifying dysfunction already present in engineering teams, particularly around mentorship and career development. The systemic issue is that networking alone can't create more jobs — the problem is structural and predates AI tools.
