> "A leader is like a shepherd. He stays behind the flock, letting the most nimble go out ahead, whereupon the others follow, not realizing that all along they are being directed from behind."

Nelson Mandela, *Long Walk to Freedom* (1994). A formulação condensada "Lead from the back — and let others believe they are in front" circula amplamente como variante editorial desta passagem.

Fonte: *Long Walk to Freedom* — Nelson Mandela (1994)
