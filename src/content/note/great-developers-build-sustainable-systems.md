**00:11:28** — Great Developers Build Sustainable Readable Systems

Practical communication in code means taking extra time to select proper variable names and writing out full words rather than abbreviations. Great developers are not chasing groundbreaking algorithms; they are building systems that deliver immense value and are sustainable. A well-communicated codebase is one that is stable, secure, easily transferred to another developer, and can be modified with little overhead. When new developers can come in and maintain that software while preserving its stability, that is what good development truly looks like.
